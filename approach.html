Approach

My work sits at the intersection of content, search, customer engagement, and process. These are often treated as separate problems. In practice, they are parts of the same system.

A typical engagement begins by identifying where momentum is being lost — in visibility, engagement, conversion, or the customer journey itself. From there, improvements are made in a focused, practical way: clearer content, stronger search structure, better pathways, and, where appropriate, conversational interfaces.

The aim is not to add complexity. It is to remove friction.

Most of my work is remote, focused, and practical. I work on a retainer or project basis, with a preference for steady improvement rather than noisy activity.

The work is iterative. Changes are introduced, observed, and refined. The emphasis is always on what is working in reality, rather than what should work in theory.

Experience

My background spans IT, consulting, finance, digital marketing, chatbot design, content, SEO, and business process improvement. That mix allows me to look across a business system across its full context — not just the content, not just the technical structure, not just the customer journey, but all of it at once — and see where the system is losing momentum.

More recently, the work has centred on digital environments — chatbot design and management, SEO-led content strategy, multi-site platform development, and AI workflow integration designed to streamline production, improve responsiveness, and reduce manual effort.

The thread running through it all is practical intelligence applied to real problems, without unnecessary complication.

Where Value Is Created

Most gains come from alignment rather than invention. When content, search, and engagement are working together, results tend to follow. When they are disconnected, effort is often wasted.

My role is to bring those elements into alignment in a way that is sustainable — capable of improving over time rather than dependent on constant intervention.
